# Receipt Upload Guidelines

## Overview
This guide helps students and parents upload payment receipts correctly for faster verification by the administration.

## What Makes a Good Receipt Photo?

### ✅ DO:
1. **Use Good Lighting**
   - Take photos in well-lit areas
   - Avoid shadows on the receipt
   - Natural daylight works best

2. **Keep Receipt Flat**
   - Place receipt on a flat surface
   - Smooth out any wrinkles or folds
   - Ensure all corners are visible

3. **Focus Properly**
   - Make sure all text is sharp and readable
   - Hold camera steady or use a surface
   - Avoid blurry images

4. **Include All Information**
   - Capture the entire receipt
   - Include all four corners
   - Don't crop important details

5. **Use High Resolution**
   - Minimum 800x600 pixels recommended
   - Higher resolution is better
   - Most modern phones are sufficient

### ❌ DON'T:
1. **Avoid Poor Lighting**
   - Don't take photos in dark areas
   - Avoid harsh shadows
   - Don't use flash if it creates glare

2. **Don't Submit Damaged Receipts**
   - Avoid torn or crumpled receipts
   - Don't submit faded receipts
   - Keep receipts in good condition

3. **Avoid Blurry Images**
   - Don't move while taking photo
   - Don't zoom in too much
   - Ensure camera is focused

4. **Don't Crop Important Parts**
   - Include receipt header
   - Include receipt footer
   - Show all transaction details

## Required Information on Receipt

Your receipt MUST include:
- ✓ Payment amount
- ✓ Payment date
- ✓ Payment method (cash/cheque/transfer)
- ✓ Issuing organization/bank name
- ✓ Receipt number or transaction ID
- ✓ Your name or student name (if applicable)

## Accepted File Formats

- **JPEG/JPG** - Recommended for photos
- **PNG** - Good for scanned documents
- **PDF** - Acceptable for digital receipts

**Maximum file size:** 5 MB

## Common Rejection Reasons

### 1. Unclear Image (40%)
- Blurry or out of focus
- Too dark or too bright
- Text not readable

**Solution:** Retake photo in good lighting with steady hand

### 2. Amount Mismatch (25%)
- Amount on receipt doesn't match entered amount
- Partial payment not indicated
- Currency mismatch

**Solution:** Double-check amount before submitting

### 3. Invalid Receipt (20%)
- Receipt from wrong institution
- Receipt for different purpose
- Expired or old receipt

**Solution:** Ensure receipt is for school fees

### 4. Missing Details (10%)
- Receipt number not visible
- Date not clear
- Student name missing

**Solution:** Include complete receipt with all details

### 5. Other Issues (5%)
- Duplicate submission
- Wrong file format
- Corrupted file

**Solution:** Follow guidelines carefully

## Step-by-Step Upload Process

### Step 1: Prepare Your Receipt
1. Ensure receipt is complete and undamaged
2. Have all payment details ready
3. Check that receipt is for correct fee type

### Step 2: Take a Good Photo
1. Place receipt on flat, well-lit surface
2. Hold camera directly above receipt
3. Ensure all corners are visible in frame
4. Take photo when camera is focused
5. Review photo before proceeding

### Step 3: Fill Out Form
1. Select correct fee structure
2. Enter exact amount from receipt
3. Enter payment date from receipt
4. Select payment method
5. Add transaction reference if available
6. Add any relevant remarks

### Step 4: Upload Receipt
1. Click "Upload" or "Take Photo with Camera"
2. Select your receipt photo
3. Review preview and rotate if needed
4. Check image quality warnings
5. Confirm all details are correct
6. Submit for verification

### Step 5: Track Status
1. Save your reference number
2. Check notification bell for updates
3. View receipt history page
4. Wait for verification (usually 1-3 days)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does verification take?
**A:** Usually 1-3 business days. You'll receive a notification when verified.

### Q: Can I upload multiple receipts at once?
**A:** No, upload one receipt per fee structure. Submit separately for different fees.

### Q: What if my receipt is rejected?
**A:** You'll receive a notification with the reason. You can upload a new receipt immediately.

### Q: Can I edit details after submission?
**A:** No, but you can upload a new receipt if rejected. Ensure details are correct before submitting.

### Q: What if I don't have a physical receipt?
**A:** Digital receipts (email confirmations, bank statements) are acceptable if they show all required information.

### Q: My receipt is in a different language. Is that okay?
**A:** Contact the finance office if your receipt is not in English. They may require translation.

### Q: Can I submit a bank statement instead?
**A:** Yes, if it clearly shows the payment to the school with all required details.

### Q: What if the amount on my receipt is different from the fee structure?
**A:** This is acceptable for partial payments. Add a remark explaining the difference.

### Q: How do I know if my receipt was received?
**A:** You'll receive a reference number immediately after upload. Save this for tracking.

### Q: Can I delete a submitted receipt?
**A:** No, but you can upload a new one if rejected. Contact admin for urgent corrections.
