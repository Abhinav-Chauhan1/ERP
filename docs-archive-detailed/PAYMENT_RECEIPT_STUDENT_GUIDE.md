# How to Upload Payment Receipts - Student Guide

## Overview

This guide will help you upload payment receipts for offline fee payments (cash, cheque, or bank transfer) through the student portal. Once uploaded, your receipt will be reviewed and verified by the school administration.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:
- A clear photo or scan of your payment receipt
- The receipt file in JPEG, PNG, or PDF format
- The file size is 5MB or less
- Your payment details (amount, date, payment method)

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access the Upload Receipt Page

1. Log in to your student portal
2. Navigate to **Fees & Payments** from the main menu
3. Click on **Upload Receipt** button in the Payment Options section

   *Note: If you don't see the Upload Receipt option, offline payment verification may not be enabled. Contact your school administration.*

### Step 2: Fill in Payment Details

Complete the following required fields:

1. **Fee Structure**: Select the fee type you're paying for (e.g., Tuition Fee, Library Fee)
2. **Payment Amount**: Enter the exact amount you paid
3. **Payment Date**: Select the date when you made the payment (cannot be a future date)
4. **Payment Method**: Choose from:
   - Cash
   - Cheque
   - Bank Transfer

### Step 3: Add Optional Information

You can also provide:
- **Transaction Reference**: Any reference number from your bank or receipt
- **Remarks**: Additional notes about the payment

### Step 4: Upload Receipt Image

1. Click on the **Upload Receipt** area or drag and drop your file
2. Select your receipt image/PDF from your device
3. Preview the uploaded file to ensure it's clear and readable
4. Make sure all payment details are visible in the image

**Tips for a Good Receipt Image:**
- Ensure good lighting and no shadows
- Keep the receipt flat and in focus
- Include all corners of the receipt
- Make sure text is readable
- Avoid blurry or dark images

### Step 5: Submit Your Receipt

1. Review all the information you've entered
2. Click the **Submit Receipt** button
3. Wait for the upload to complete (you'll see a progress indicator)
4. Once successful, you'll receive a **reference number** - save this for tracking

## After Submission

### Tracking Your Receipt Status

1. Go to **Fees & Payments** > **Receipt History**
2. Find your uploaded receipt using the reference number
3. Check the status:
   - **Pending Verification**: Your receipt is awaiting admin review
   - **Verified**: Your payment has been approved and recorded
   - **Rejected**: Your receipt was not approved (see rejection reason)

### Receipt Status Meanings

**Pending Verification** (⏱️)
- Your receipt has been submitted successfully
- It's in the queue for admin review
- No action needed from you at this time
- Typical review time: 1-3 business days

**Verified** (✓)
- Your payment has been approved
- Your fee balance has been updated
- You'll receive a notification confirming verification
- The payment is now part of your official payment history

**Rejected** (✗)
- Your receipt could not be verified
- Check the rejection reason provided
- Common reasons include:
  - Receipt image is unclear or unreadable
  - Payment details don't match the receipt
  - Receipt is for a different student
  - Duplicate submission
- You can upload a new receipt with corrections

### Notifications

You'll receive notifications when:
- Your receipt is verified (includes updated balance)
- Your receipt is rejected (includes reason for rejection)

Check your notifications regularly or enable email notifications in your settings.

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue: "File size too large"
**Solution**: Compress your image or use a lower resolution. Maximum file size is 5MB.

### Issue: "Invalid file format"
**Solution**: Convert your file to JPEG, PNG, or PDF format.

### Issue: "Payment date cannot be in the future"
**Solution**: Double-check the date you selected. It must be today or earlier.

### Issue: "Receipt image is unclear"
**Solution**: 
- Retake the photo in better lighting
- Ensure the receipt is flat and in focus
- Try scanning instead of photographing
- Make sure all text is readable

### Issue: Receipt was rejected
**Solution**:
1. Read the rejection reason carefully
2. Address the specific issue mentioned
3. Upload a new receipt with corrections
4. Contact administration if you need clarification

## Best Practices

1. **Upload Promptly**: Submit receipts within 24-48 hours of payment
2. **Keep Originals**: Retain physical receipts until verification is complete
3. **Double-Check Details**: Verify all information before submitting
4. **Clear Images**: Take time to capture a clear, readable image
5. **Track Status**: Check your receipt status regularly
6. **Save Reference Number**: Keep your reference number for tracking

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does verification take?
**A:** Typically 1-3 business days. During peak periods (start of term), it may take longer.

### Q: Can I upload multiple receipts at once?
**A:** No, you need to upload each receipt separately with its own payment details.

### Q: What if I made a mistake in the details?
**A:** Contact the administration immediately. Once submitted, you cannot edit the details.

### Q: Can I delete an uploaded receipt?
**A:** No, once uploaded, receipts cannot be deleted. If you uploaded incorrectly, contact administration.

### Q: What if my receipt is rejected?
**A:** Read the rejection reason, correct the issue, and upload a new receipt. You can also contact administration for clarification.

### Q: Will my fee balance update immediately?
**A:** No, your balance updates only after an administrator verifies your receipt.

### Q: Can I pay partial amounts?
**A:** Yes, you can upload receipts for partial payments. Just enter the exact amount you paid.

### Q: What if I don't have a receipt?
**A:** Contact the school administration. They may be able to manually record your payment with proper verification.

### Q: Is my receipt information secure?
**A:** Yes, all receipt images are stored securely with encryption, and only you and authorized administrators can view them.

### Q: Can my parents upload receipts for me?
**A:** Yes, parents can upload receipts through the parent portal by selecting your name.
